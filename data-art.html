"Orbiting Petals: A Cosmic Dance of Near-Earth Objects"

"This interactive data artwork offers a visual exploration of Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) gathered from NASA's public API. At the heart of the piece is Earth, symbolized by a blue sphere, around which lines and dots extend out like petals on a flower. Each line represents the distance of a NEO from Earth, and each dot at the end of the line indicates the NEO's size relative to our planet. The varying lengths and sizes seek to invoke both awe and contemplation, highlighting the multitude of celestial bodies that come alarmingly close to our world. The artwork not only serves as an aesthetic venture but also as an educational tool, giving viewers a new perspective on the universe we inhabit."
